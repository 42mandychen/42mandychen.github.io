More About Me

Since you’re reading this part, I assume you want to know more about me. I guess I’ll get a little bit more personal here.

I like taking photos. I don’t have professional cameras and lens, so all my photos were taken with my 2-year-old phone. As I’m walking around everyday mostly on UBC Vancouver campus, I took a number of pictures of UBC. Taking photos is my way of recording life. If you’re interested in seeing my life through my eyes, check out my Instagram. Feel free to take a look at my collection of food pictures here too, if you are not hungry at the moment. Otherwise I don't suggest doing that as my website is not edible.

I'm taking introductory level Japanese courses. I used to watch a lot of Japanese dramas, but not anymore as I don’t have that much time. I also watch some animes, surprisingly, with my dad. He is into animes too! Overall, me and my family are just very interested in Japanese culture. I did take a Japanese culture course (ANTH 215) at UBC, although that didn’t end up quite well in terms of how fun it was.

I love Markdown. Markdown is simple, yet so beautiful. This site was supposed to be built using Jekyll, but after wrestling with it for a few hours (can’t install, can’t push, can’t pull …) I gave up. Newer Mac systems have some strict security settings that make Jekyll installation extremely frustrating. Oh well. But you can still check out my class notes in Markdown.
